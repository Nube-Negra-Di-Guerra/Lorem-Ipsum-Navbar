"Lorem Ipsum"

Lorem Ipsum is a Latin phrase that roughly translates to "pain itself." The phrase has no real meaning. It originated in the 16th century as a scrambled version of a passage from the book "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" by the Roman philosopher and statesman Cicero.

The full Latin phrase "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" roughly translates to "About the Ends of Goods and Evils" in English.
